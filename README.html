Backdrop Generator Add-on

About

The Backdrop Generator is a powerful Blender add-on designed to enhance your scenes with stunning backgrounds and captivating lighting. With just a few clicks, it generates smooth and visually pleasing backdrops for your subjects, creating a professional and polished look. The addon offers a range of preset lighting options to beautifully illuminate your scenes. Additionally, it provides the flexibility to scatter objects in the background, bringing dynamic movement and interest to your compositions. Whether you're working on architectural visualization, product rendering, or artistic projects, the Backdrop Generator empowers you to effortlessly create visually captivating and engaging scenes.

Installation

Don't unzip this archive. To install Backdrop Generator, follow these steps:

  1. Open Blender.
  2. In the top bar, open the Edit menu.
  3. From there, open the Preferences window.
  4. On the left side of the Preferences window, choose the Add-ons tab.
  5. In the top right of the Preferences window, click the Install button.
  6. Browse to the Backdrop Generator zip file you downloaded.
  7. In the bottom right of the file browser, click Install Add-on.
  8. You should now see Backdrop Generator in the list of add-ons.
  9. Tick the box to the left of the add-on name to enable it.
